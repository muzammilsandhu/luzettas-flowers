our story

Since 2008, my husband TJ and I have owned the business, raising our four children - Thomas, Zettie, Tristan, and Trent - along the way. With the help of our friends and family, we've carved out a special place in the heart of Malheur County. We're dedicated to providing you with unique, handcrafted gifts, crafting breathtaking floral arrangements, and providing the best service we can.

our values

Customer Service: We share your passion for prompt service. Our mission is to consistently exceed your expectations, ensuring every interaction with us is memorable.

Community Spirit:  TJ and I cherish our role within the community. Supporting our neighbors and sharing their special moments is the driving force behind what we do.

Emotional Connection: We take pride in being there for you during life's highs and lows, delivering solace and celebration in every petal.

our approach

We're here to listen, understand your needs, and create floral designs that eloquently convey your sentiments.

What do you need from me to create an arrangement?

When we chat about your arrangement, it's really helpful to get a sense of:

  • Your preferred colors
  • Your budget or price range (arrangements start at $40)
  • Any specific flowers or arrangements you have in mind
  • The chance to craft something uniquely beautiful, just for you
